01 Abstract

AI Recipe & Nutrition Optimizer

A multi-objective optimizer that reformulates recipes for better nutrition while preserving taste, blending nutritional science and bioavailability heuristics with constrained optimization.

02 Highlights

Methods & contributions

  • Constrained optimization — SLSQP over a USDA-style nutrition database with dietary constraints and per-serving scaling.
  • Bioavailability-aware — accounts for heme vs non-heme iron, oxalate-bound calcium, and curcumin–piperine synergy.
  • Substitution engine — ingredient swaps that satisfy AHA/DRI targets (saturated fat, sodium).
  • Meal planning — extends single-recipe optimization to multi-meal plans via a FastAPI service.
03 Architecture

Data flow

recipe → nutrition vectors → SLSQP (maximize nutrition
       s.t. taste + dietary constraints) → substitutions
